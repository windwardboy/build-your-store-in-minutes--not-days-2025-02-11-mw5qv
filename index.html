About Project Nautica

Founded in 2020 by a team of professional photographers and e-commerce experts, Project Nautica emerged from a simple observation: small businesses needed better tools to create professional product photography. Our journey began in a small studio in San Francisco, where we developed our first prototype to address the common challenges faced by online sellers.

Our mission is to democratize professional product photography by providing accessible, high-quality lighting solutions to businesses of all sizes. We believe in empowering entrepreneurs with the tools they need to compete in the digital marketplace, guided by our core values of innovation, simplicity, and customer success.
